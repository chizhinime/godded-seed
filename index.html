About the Event

Embracing the path to GREATNESS

The Godded Seed Confab is a transformative gathering designed to unlock your potential and guide you on the path to GREATNESS. This event brings together visionaries, thought leaders, and individuals committed to personal and professional excellence.

Whether you're seeking personal growth, professional development, or spiritual enlightenment, this conference offers a unique opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals and gain insights that will propel you forward in your journey.

Featured Speakers

Learn from inspiring leaders and mentors

Reigner Davies

College Director, Mould Break Institute

Reigner Davies Is The College Director Of The Mouldbreak Learning Community; A Life Coach, Mentor & Management/Leadership Developer, Sought After On Various Platforms Across The Nation As A Leading Resource In Inspirational Leadership.

Dunamis Shiloh Okonwor

President, Godded Seed Community

Dunamis Shiloh Okonwor is a polymath executive specializing in brand strategy, business growth, and social innovation. He transforms personal brands into business assets and designs high-value strategies to attract profitable clients. With expertise in growth, branding, marketing, and management, he has delivered success for leading brands in and beyond Nigeria. Driven by a vision to empower Africans, he inspires thinking beyond skin color to solve global problems through business and innovation.

Joshua Triumphant

Product Manager, Leadership Development Trainer

Joshua Triumphant is a maverick, mentor, and dynamic leader, often described as a thought disruptor with a brilliant mind. Passionate about Africa’s growth, he envisions the continent thriving with intentional support for its people. Believing young and middle-aged leaders will shape the future in unconventional ways, he dedicates himself to guiding and expanding their vision beyond traditional leadership limits.

Kobaba Jornsen

Mayor of Pitakwa

Kobaba, one of Nigeria’s finest multi-award-winning comedians based in Port Harcourt, is known for clean, classy, and original humor. Articulate, spontaneous, and entrepreneurial, he holds a degree in marketing and has created notable entertainment brands, including Kobaba on Duty, The Mayor of Pitakwa, Gospel Gang Party, Gospel Gang Carnival, and Kobaba Youth Convention.
